Who We Are

Rooted in purpose,
growing with every tree

Bloom began as a small group of friends with a shared conviction: that ordinary people, working together, can restore the natural world. Since our founding we have grown into a registered non-profit mobilizing thousands of volunteers across the country — and we are just getting started.

Our Journey

From a small idea to a national movement

Every milestone below marks a moment that shaped who Bloom is today.

2015
founding-2015.jpg
Founded

Bloom is born

Four friends plant 30 native oak saplings in a disused field outside Athens. The idea: build a community-led reforestation movement from the ground up, one tree at a time.

2017
registered-2017.jpg
Milestone

Registered as a non-profit

After two years of grassroots activity Bloom receives official charitable status, unlocking grant funding and enabling us to scale our planting operations nationally.

2019
event-2019.jpg
Growth

First national planting day

Over 400 volunteers converge on six sites simultaneously across Greece. In a single weekend Bloom plants more than 10,000 trees — our largest single event to that point.

2021
platform-2021.jpg
Technology

The platform launches

Bloom goes digital — a volunteer coordination platform connects site leaders with contributors for the first time, removing the coordination bottleneck that had slowed growth.

2023
fifty-thousand-2023.jpg
Milestone

50,000 trees planted

A landmark moment — Bloom's cumulative planting total crosses 50,000 trees across 10 active restoration sites. Wildfire response becomes a core part of our mission.

2026
today-2026.jpg
Today

Building for scale

Bloom's e-commerce platform goes live — every purchase plants trees automatically. We are now seeking corporate partners to fund the next phase of restoration across Greece.
