Our Promise

How You Can Help

Every action leaves a mark

Whether you have an afternoon or a lifetime, there is a place for you in the Bloom community.

Tree Planting Events

Join one of our weekend planting days at sites across the country. No experience needed — just a willingness to get your hands in the soil.

Shop for a Cause

Every product in our store plants trees automatically. A Classic Tee plants 90 trees. A Hoodie plants 190. Shop and make an impact without lifting a shovel.

Make a Direct Donation

Every dollar you donate plants 5 trees — instantly logged and counted toward our live total. 100% of donations go directly to reforestation.

Track Your Impact

Create an account and every purchase and donation is tracked in your personal dashboard. Watch your tree total grow with every contribution.

See Real Results

Our impact page shows live before-and-after restoration photos, CO₂ offset calculations, and a running total of every tree planted through the platform.

Corporate Partnerships

Looking for meaningful ESG impact? Partner with Bloom. Your brand gets co-branding, verified quarterly impact reports, and a live platform counting your trees.
